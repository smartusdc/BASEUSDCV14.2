Security Best Practices

Protect your investment by following these security guidelines:

  • Always verify the contract address on BaseScan before interacting
  • Connect only through the official website (baseusdc.com)
  • Keep your private keys and seed phrases secure and never share them
  • Monitor your staking activity regularly
  • Maintain sufficient ETH balance for withdrawal transactions

Transaction Safety

Follow these guidelines for safe transactions:

  • Review all transaction details carefully before confirming
  • Verify gas fees are within normal range (under $0.10)
  • Start with small test transactions when unsure
  • Keep records of your transactions for reference
  • Check smart contract verification status on BaseScan

Troubleshooting Guide

Before contacting support, please review these common issues and their solutions. Most problems can be resolved using this guide.

Transaction Issues

Transaction Pending for Long Time

When a transaction remains pending for an extended period:

  1. Check MetaMask for pending transaction notifications
  2. If the transaction is stuck:
    • Use the "Speed Up" option with a slightly higher gas price
    • Cancel the transaction if it hasn't been mined
    • Reset MetaMask activity if necessary

Note: Base Network transactions typically confirm within 15 seconds. If a transaction is pending longer than 2 minutes, action may be needed.

Insufficient Gas Fee Errors

Error: [ethjs-query] while formatting outputs from RPC '{"value":{"code":-32603,"data":{"code":-32000,"message":"insufficient funds for gas"}}}'
Solution:

This error occurs when your wallet lacks sufficient ETH for transaction fees. To resolve this issue:

  1. Ensure you have at least $1-2 worth of ETH in your wallet specifically for gas fees on Base Network
  2. Transfer additional ETH from Coinbase to your MetaMask wallet if needed
  3. Wait for lower gas prices if they are unusually high (normal gas fees on Base are under $0.10)

USDC Approval Issues

USDC Approval Not Working

Common Causes and Solutions:
  1. Check Your Balance:
    • Verify your USDC balance on Base Network
    • Ensure you're not checking balances on other networks
  2. Reset Approval Process:
    • Try approving a smaller amount first
    • Wait 10 minutes if a previous approval is pending
    • Verify you have sufficient ETH for the approval transaction

Reward Calculation and Claiming

Rewards Not Showing or Incorrect

Understanding Reward Calculations:

Your rewards are calculated based on several factors:

  • Your total staked USDC amount
  • Current platform APR rate
  • Time elapsed since last claim or deposit
  • Any applicable referral bonuses

If your rewards appear incorrect:

  1. Refresh the page and reconnect your wallet
  2. Verify your staking duration
  3. Check for any pending deposits
  4. Confirm your referral status if applicable

Emergency Solutions

Unable to Withdraw Funds

If you're experiencing withdrawal issues:
  1. Verify Contract Status:
    • Check the contract on BaseScan
    • Ensure withdrawals are not temporarily paused
    • Verify you have sufficient staked balance
  2. Technical Requirements:
    • Maintain sufficient ETH for gas fees
    • Try withdrawing a smaller amount first
    • Double-check your connected wallet address
