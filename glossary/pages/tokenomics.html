Economics

Protocol Token Economics

The economic design principles and mechanisms that govern token creation, distribution, and utility within DeFi protocols. Well-designed tokenomics ensure long-term sustainability and align stakeholder interests.

Key Components:
  • Token Supply Mechanics
    - Initial distribution model
    - Emission schedule
    - Supply caps and inflation
    - Burning mechanisms
  • Value Drivers
    - Utility functions
    - Governance rights
    - Revenue sharing
    - Protocol incentives
  • Market Dynamics
    - Lock-up periods
    - Vesting schedules
    - Liquidity incentives
    - Price stability mechanisms
Economic Model Example:
Token Distribution:
├── Protocol Treasury: 30%
├── Community Rewards: 40%
├── Development Fund: 20%
└── Early Supporters: 10%

Vesting Schedule:
Year 1: 25% unlock
Year 2-4: Linear vesting

    - Performance incentives
  • Participation Rewards

    - Protocol usage rewards
    - Referral programs
    - Community contributions
  • Risk-Adjusted Returns
    - Risk-based rewards
    - Duration multipliers
    - Volume incentives
    - Loyalty programs
